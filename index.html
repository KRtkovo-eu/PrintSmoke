Are you a smoker? Do you want to avoid hawking but keep smokin'?

Use PrintSmoke!

PrintSmoke is free to download*, cheap to print and simple to use.

Download

PrintSmoke is completely free to download*, all sources are stored in GitHub repository.

Customize

PrintSmoke is fully customizable. According to license, each part can be redesigned.

Print (or buy)

PrintSmoke is prepared for 3D printing. Print it on your own machine, find a nearest printing hub or visit our store (will be soon).

Smoke

PrintSmoke + charcoal filters + clean pipe + some smelly shit = the best taste!
It's compatible with 8mm Ø charcoal filters (e.g. actiTube filters) and glass pipes or joints (6-7mm Ø).
